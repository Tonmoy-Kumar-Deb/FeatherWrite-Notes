Privacy Policy

At FeatherWrite Notes, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as your name, department, course, roll number, WhatsApp number, and work details when you apply for our services. This information is used solely for order processing and communication.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to process your order, track payments, and deliver your work. We do not share your information with any third party unless required by law.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. All data is stored securely and is only accessible to authorized personnel.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected. After your work is delivered and payments are settled, your data may be anonymized or deleted as per our data retention policy.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. If you have any concerns, please contact us via WhatsApp or email.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated through our website or application.

7. Contact

For any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please contact us via WhatsApp or Instagram or email as provided in the application.
